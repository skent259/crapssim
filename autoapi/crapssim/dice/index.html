crapssim.dice

The dice are used by the craps Table for keeping track of the latest roll and the total number of rolls so far. The dice object is mostly handled internally, but advanced users may access it (through the Table, as table.dice) for new bets or strategies as needed.

Attributes

DicePair

Pair of dice represented as (die_one, die_two).

DicePairInput

Pair of dice represented as an iterable of two integers.

The randomness of the dice is based on numpy.random, which uses the PCG-64 pseudo-random number generation (see numpy.random.PCG64`).
